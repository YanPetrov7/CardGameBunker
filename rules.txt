Each player has two goals:

Personal - get into the bunker. 
Command - to ensure that only healthy and fit for survival people get into the bunker. During the game, you gradually reveal your character, show strong cards and explain why you should survive.

Each player has two action cards, they could save your life in bunker or ruin life of other players. There are cards and their definitions:

steal: cards which starts from word 'steal' give you an ability to exchange your card whith card of other player;
show: cards which starts from word 'show' give you an ability to show characteristic of other player;
reroll: card which starts from word 'reroll' give you an ability to change characteristic of all players in this game;
